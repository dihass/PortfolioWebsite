# PRODUCT.md — Dihas Sathnindu Portfolio

## Product Purpose
A personal developer portfolio targeting junior software engineering recruiters and hiring managers. The deliverable is the design itself — a visitor's impression is the product. Must communicate technical depth, real-world shipping experience, and personality within seconds.

## Register
brand

## Users
- Primary: Technical recruiters and hiring managers at software companies
- Secondary: Engineers reviewing candidates for junior SWE roles
- Tertiary: Potential collaborators, freelance clients

## Brand Voice
Three words: nocturnal, precise, human.

Physical object: a late-night engineering journal — handwritten margin notes beside clean technical diagrams. Warm lamplight, not cold fluorescents.

## Anti-references
- Generic dev portfolio (blue/purple accent, centered hero, icon-grid skills)
- Brutalist terminal aesthetic (too cold, no warmth)
- Maximalist anime (too chaotic for professional context)
- Pastel kawaii (too soft, undermines technical credibility)

## Strategic Principles
- Lead with technical depth, not personality
- DarvinCode: mention subtly — shows entrepreneurial spirit, not a distraction
- Every section earns its place: if a recruiter spends 90 seconds, what must they see?
- The Seascape and Sepsis Detection are the two strongest projects — frame everything around them
